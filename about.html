Who We Are

Project Readiness & Finance Consultants (PRFC), Khyber Pakhtunkhwa is a cutting-edge engineering and geospatial intelligence firm delivering sustainable, tech-driven infrastructure solutions.

Our Vision

Revolutionizing Pakistan's infrastructure and environmental planning by merging engineering excellence with digital geospatial innovation.

What We Do

Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

Real-time spatial intelligence

  • Real-time dashboards for infrastructure monitoring
  • Spatial databases for efficient resource management
  • Web GIS solutions for collaborative planning

Remote Sensing

Advanced environmental analytics

  • Satellite/drone analytics for hydrology studies
  • Snow melt monitoring for water resource planning
  • Land use planning with precision agriculture

Civil Engineering

Sustainable infrastructure development

  • Smart irrigation systems for efficient water use
  • Modern canal systems and water supply networks
  • Rural infrastructure development

AI + IoT Integration

Smart monitoring solutions

  • Smart sensors for infrastructure health monitoring
  • Predictive maintenance forecasting
  • Real-time data analytics for decision making
